About Me

I am a computationally minded astrophysics student working at the interface of galaxy dynamics and small-scale structure formation. Recently I built a full pipeline on IllustrisTNG to match hydrodynamic and DMO runs and measure how baryons change infall times, orbital shapes, and collision angles of satellite mergers. Earlier, I led a viscoelastic-flow project (FENE-P in OpenFOAM) that became a first-author paper in Physics of Fluids (2025).

  • 1× first-author paper published: Physics of Fluids (2025)
  • 1× co-authored submission in review (PoF, viscoelastic convection with embedded cylinders)
  • Galaxy-merger paper draft targeting Research in Astronomy and Astrophysics
