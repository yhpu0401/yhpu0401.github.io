My Hobbies

Outside of academics and research, I enjoy activities that combine creativity, movement, focus, and self-expression.

Figure Skating

Figure skating is one of my favorite hobbies. I love the feeling of speed and freedom on the ice, as well as the combination of athleticism and artistic expression. Skating allows me to challenge myself while enjoying music and movement.

Tennis

Tennis helps me stay active and focused. I enjoy the fast pace of the game, the strategy behind every match, and the process of continuously improving my technique and endurance.

Swimming

Swimming is both relaxing and refreshing for me. It allows me to clear my mind while improving physical strength and stamina. I especially enjoy the calm feeling of being in the water.

Painting

Painting gives me a way to express creativity and emotions. I enjoy experimenting with colors and different artistic styles, and it helps me relax outside of daily academic work.
